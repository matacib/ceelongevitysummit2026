Program Architecture

Three days. Three audiences. One longevity ecosystem.

The program moves from clinical depth to public-facing lifestyle systems and concludes with immersive retreat formats at x-bionic® sphere.

Day 1 · Wednesday, October 29

Clinical Longevity

From blood panels to biological age — how to build a longevity practice. Diagnostics, biomarkers, AI in preventive medicine, clinical protocols and a panel on running a successful longevity clinic. VIP Welcome Dinner in the evening.

Day 2 · Thursday, October 30

Biohacking & Beauty

Where health optimization meets aesthetics. Skin longevity as a biomarker, wearables and HRV tracking, cellular regeneration, microbiome-driven beauty, functional nutrition and live technology demos from partners. Networking Dinner and Film Screening.

Day 3 · Friday, October 31

Lifestyle

The science of daily practice. Breathwork, deliberate cold and heat exposure, precision nutrition, sleep optimization, athlete-led training sessions and hands-on workshops. Expo activations and closing ceremony.

Venue

x-bionic® sphere, Šamorín

One million square metres of sport, science and recovery on the banks of the Danube. x-bionic® sphere is Slovakia's Olympic Training Center — a resort built for elite athletes that now opens its doors to the longevity community. The 282-room hotel, seven-sauna wellness complex, Olympic swimming pool, athletic stadium and 600+ capacity congress center sit under one roof, creating a venue where conference programming and embodied health experience are inseparable.

Located on the Danube, minutes from Bratislava and less than an hour from Vienna Airport, the resort creates a rare setting for a summit that combines high-level conference programming with embodied longevity experience. Every European capital is reachable within three hours.
