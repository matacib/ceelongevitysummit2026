1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, tablet or phone) when you visit them. They allow websites to remember your actions and preferences over time and to function properly.

Some cookies are essential for the website to work, while others help us understand how visitors use the site and provide a more personalized experience.
